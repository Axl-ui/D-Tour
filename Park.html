About People's Park

People’s Park, located in the heart of Davao City, Philippines, is a vibrant urban park known for its lush greenery, unique sculptures, and family-friendly atmosphere. This iconic landmark features a diverse array of attractions, including a durian-inspired dome, colorful gardens, a dancing fountain, and life-sized indigenous-themed statues. It serves as a hub for relaxation, cultural appreciation, and outdoor activities. The park also hosts jogging paths and open spaces perfect for leisure and recreation. Easily accessible and free to the public, People’s Park is a favorite destination for locals and tourists alike, offering a peaceful escape within the bustling city.

Getting to People's Park

Getting to People’s Park is simple and convenient, as it is centrally located in Davao City. Visitors can reach the park by jeepney, taxi, or private vehicle. Most public transport routes passing through downtown Davao include stops near the park. For those staying in nearby hotels, walking to People’s Park is an option, as it is easily accessible within the city’s business district. Once you arrive, the park’s vibrant entrance and welcoming atmosphere make it easy to spot and enjoy.
