About Panas Falls

Panas Falls, located in New Corella, Davao del Norte, Philippines, is a breathtaking natural attraction known for its cascading waters and serene ambiance. Surrounded by lush greenery, the falls are composed of multiple tiers, creating picturesque pools perfect for swimming, wading, and relaxing. The tranquil environment makes it an ideal getaway for nature enthusiasts and those seeking a peaceful escape from the city.

Getting to Panas Falls

Getting to Panas Falls is convenient and rewarding for nature lovers. From Davao City, travelers can take a bus or van heading to New Corella, Davao del Norte, with the journey taking about 1.5 to 2 hours. Once in New Corella, local transportation such as motorcycles (habal-habal) or tricycles can take you directly to the falls. The road to Panas Falls includes a mix of paved and rough terrain, adding a touch of adventure to the trip. Upon arrival, visitors are greeted by the soothing sounds of cascading water and the lush natural surroundings, making the journey well worth it.
